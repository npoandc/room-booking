Room & Desk Booking

A simple, step-by-step guide for everyone
booking.oliverandco.co.uk Open this in your web browser to start

1. Opening the booking page

  1. Open your web browser (Edge, Chrome or Safari).
  2. Go to booking.oliverandco.co.uk
  3. You will be asked to sign in — use your Oliver & Co Microsoft account (the same email and password you use for Outlook).
  4. Once signed in, the rooms for today appear straight away.
Tip: Save the page to your Favourites so you don't have to type the address each time. On a phone you can use “Add to Home Screen” to get an app-style icon.

2. Book a room

  1. Find the room you want, along its row.
  2. Click an empty white square at the time you want.
  3. A box opens. Choose the From and To times, type the Purpose, and type Your name.
  4. If you need to leave extra information (e.g. “parking space needed” or “set up chairs for 8”), type it in the Notes box. This is optional.
  5. Click the pink Book room button.

Done — your booking appears on the grid in the room's colour.

3. If the room is already taken

You can never double-book by mistake. If the room is busy at that time, the app shows a yellow warning and tells you who has it. Just pick another time or another room.

4. Change or cancel a booking

  1. Click the booking you want to change.
  2. A box opens showing the details.
  3. Click Change… to move it, or Cancel… to remove it.
  4. Type who you are and the reason.
  5. Click Confirm.
Please note: a name and a reason are always required for any change or cancellation. This is saved so everyone can see what happened and why.

After the change or cancellation goes through, a small pop-up appears asking if you want to notify someone by email.

  1. Type the recipient’s email address and click Send email — Outlook will open with a ready-to-send message containing the booking details.
  2. Click Skip if you don’t need to notify anyone.

5. See a whole week or month

At the top of the page there are three buttons: Day, Week and Month. Click one to change the view. In Week or Month, click any day to open it for booking.

6. Put a booking in your own calendar

  1. Click the booking.
  2. Click Add to my calendar.
  3. Click Outlook (work). The event opens — click Save in Outlook.

7. Booking for someone else

Assistants can book on behalf of others:

  1. When booking, type their Oliver & Co email in the Invite people by email box.
  2. Click Book room.
  3. Your email program opens a ready-made invite — just press Send.
  4. They click the link in the email to add it to their own calendar.
Tip: invites can only be sent to @oliverandco.co.uk email addresses.

8. See who booked, changed or cancelled

Click the purple History button in the bottom-right corner. It lists every booking, change and cancellation — newest first — with who did it, why, and the date and time.

9. Booking a desk at the Hoole office

  1. Click Hoole desks → at the top of the page.
  2. Click a desk that says Free — click to book.
  3. Choose your Department and type Your name.
  4. Click Book desk.

Desks are booked for the whole day. The Department bookings panel at the bottom shows which team is using the desks the most.

10. Make a repeating booking

If you need the same room on a regular basis, the app can book every occurrence in one go.

  1. Click an empty slot to open the booking box as normal.
  2. Fill in From, To, Purpose and Your name.
  3. Open the Repeat dropdown and choose how often:
    • Every week — same day every 1, 2, 3, 4, 6 or 8 weeks.
    • Monthly (set day) — e.g. the 3rd Friday of every month.
  4. If you chose Monthly (set day), two extra dropdowns appear:
    • Which — 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th or Last.
    • Day — Monday through Friday.
  5. Choose the Last date — this is when the repeat should stop (up to one year ahead).
  6. Click Book room. All the dates are created at once.
Tip: If one of the recurring dates clashes with an existing booking, that date is skipped automatically and you are told which ones were missed.
Cancelling a series: click any booking in the series, then click Cancel… and choose Cancel this & all future to remove the rest of the series in one go.

11. Search for a booking

Use the Search box at the top of the page to find any booking by purpose or by the person's name.

  1. Click the Search… box and start typing — for example a surname or a meeting name.
  2. Matching bookings are highlighted with a yellow outline. The active match has a pink outline.
  3. A counter appears (e.g. 1 / 3) showing which result you are on.
  4. Use the ‹ and › arrows next to the counter to jump to the previous or next match.
  5. Clear the search box to remove the highlights and go back to normal.
Tip: Search works in Day, Week and Month view. In Week or Month view you may see several highlighted bookings at once — use the arrows to step through them.

Remember: rooms and desks can be booked Monday to Friday, 8:00 am to 6:00 pm. Every change and cancellation needs a name and a reason.

Any problems with the app? Speak to the IT or People team.
